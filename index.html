About Us

Years of quality service, a well-maintained distributor network

B.D. Edible oils

B.D. Edible Oils Pvt. Ltd, commenced its journey in 1990 and over a period of four decades it has become the leading manufacturer and exporter of Oil Seeds, Edible Oils and Soya Chunks in India. Our product portfolio consists of crude mustard oil, refined Groundnut and Soybean oil, and Soya Chunks & Granules. We produce and sell oils under the brand name of NETAJI® and Soya Chunks under the brand name of Active-X.
B.D. Edible Oils Pvt. Ltd owns the legacy of supplying the best Edible Oils which add taste and flavor to any food item. It has always been applauded for its purity, freshness and superior quality by customers. As a consequence, B.D. Edible Oils Pvt. Ltd has become a trusted brand across the country and abroad.
NETAJI® is a premium edible oil brand and is a household name with Indian consumers who use our products regularly as a healthy cooking medium. An experience of four decades and extensive market research on consumer taste preferences is the secret of our success.
